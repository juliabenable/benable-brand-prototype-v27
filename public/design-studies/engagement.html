What changes

  • Keep: eyebrow + 5× hero + bar (the core proof).
  • Drop: the creator-faces row. The takeaway now carries "4 handpicked creators" in the copy itself.
  • Drop: two of the three chip-quotes.
  • Promote: the single best chip-quote to a real hero quote — bigger card, attribution visible, avatar slightly larger. One honest comment reads more authentically than three small ones.
  • Promote: the Benable-sourcing footer message from 10px tucked-under to 13px bold takeaway. This IS the takeaway, not a caption.

Why

Seven competing elements means nothing lands. The viewer's eye doesn't know where to go first — it bounces between the number, the bar, the faces, the quotes, the footer.

Four well-spaced elements with clear hierarchy means each one gets its job:

  • 5× + bar — the headline proof.
  • Hero quote — the human voice.
  • Takeaway — why Benable was the reason.
