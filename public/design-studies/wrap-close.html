What changes

  • Sparkles on enter: 8–12 die-cut shapes (dots, stars, hearts) in pink/purple/gold positioned around the upper half of the slide. They scale + fade in on slide mount (staggered 0.05s delays, 0.9s curve), then gently float (3.4s loop, ±6px vertical bob).
  • Sparkles glow: each gets a soft drop-shadow in its own color so they read as bright/festive against the dark gradient.
  • Signature footer: small "made with ♥ by Benable" with a tiny gradient B-mark, bottom-center, soft-white at 50% opacity. Closes the loop without competing.
  • Everything else unchanged: "That's a wrap." heading, subtitle, and the Send-Katie CTA stay exactly where they are.

Why

After seven slides of celebration the last beat shouldn't be quiet — it should be the payoff. The sparkles deliver a small visceral moment ("oh, nice") without being noisy or cartoonish.

The signature is the brand owning the moment — saying "this whole experience was Benable" at the very end. Tiny, classy, doesn't ask for attention but reminds the viewer who built this for them.
